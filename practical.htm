DESCRIPTION

The department fosters a dynamic learning environment where theory meets practice. Classrooms buzz with discussions on algorithms, data structures, and emerging technologies like AI and cybersecurity. Labs are equipped with modern systems, and students often huddle around whiteboards sketching out logic flows or debugging code together.
Students here are known for their collaborative spirit. Whether it's preparing for programming contests, building apps, or contributing to open-source projects, there's a palpable energy of creation. The department regularly hosts seminars, workshops, and hackathons, inviting industry experts and alumni to share insights and inspire the next generation.

COURSES

  • Programming & Software Development
  • Algorithms & Data Structures
  • Networking & Security
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Systems & Architecture
  • Databases & Data Science

FACILITES

  • Internet and campus-wide Wi-Fi
  • Dedicated Computer Labs for programming and system-level tasks
  • Networking Lab with routers, switches, and simulation tools
  • AI & Machine Learning Lab with GPU-enabled systems
  • Project Rooms for group work and final-year projects
  • Seminars, workshops, and hackathons for industry exposure
